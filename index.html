About Us

Synthbiosys is a digital health consultancy and software development company that provides deep technology solutions focused on removing barriers for those living with disabilities.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to provide access to high-quality and affordable health care for people with disabilities, through new health care options and assistive technology tools for their inclusion in all aspects of everyday life.

  • Business concept

    Synthbiosys specializes in the development and implementation of deep technology solutions in health-related support services and personalized medicine for disability inclusion.

    We are developing artificial intelligence software, virtual reality environments, brain-computer interfaces, and computer vision systems to create revolutionary new solutions to improve accessibility and quality of care for patients.

  • Who is involved?

    A core component of Synthbiosys is the use of multidisciplinary teams. Our team-based approach allows us to develop customized and data-driven patient care plans for improved efficiency and better outcomes.
